PDG Meeting Records (README)

USAGE

Who maintains it?

MSDL PDG Secretary (Ken Peplow)

Intent

Compilations of all PDG (not drafting group) meeting artifacts.

Specific Guidance

Associate all records with document "MSDL Meeting Artifacts"

Each meeting has its own subfolder named by convention

YYYYMMDDtypfmt

where:

FIELDVALUE
YYYY four digit year
MM two digit month 1-12
DD two digit day of month 00-31
typ three character enumeration of meeting type(per ROR)
fmt three character enumeration of meeting format(per SISO)

and

typfmt
  1. Reg - A regularly scheduled meeting
  2. Spc - Special meeting like kickoff or calling for an electronic vote
  1. Tel - Teleconference
  2. F2f - Face to Face
  3. Web - Web reflector

Example:

The PDG Kickoff held on April 5, 2006, was a special meeting and was face to face, hence:

20060405SpcF2f


Sub Folder Content

One subfolder maintained per meeting containing:

  • Agenda
  • Slides
  • Minutes
  • Other documents not referenced elsewhere
